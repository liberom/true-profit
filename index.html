How it works

The calculator subtracts cost of goods, shipping, marketplace percentage fees, and payment processing (percentage + fixed) from the sale price to compute your net profit and profit margin.

Default fees are set to Stripe's typical 2.9% + $0.30 and a marketplace fee of 6.5% (Etsy example). Adjust values to match your platform.
